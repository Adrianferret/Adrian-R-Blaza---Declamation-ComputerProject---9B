When I look at humanity, I see.. a garden!
An amalgamation of flowers!
But this is what gives it it's beauty.
All these different Daisies, Sunflowers, Roses
Working together to create this wondrous display of unity!
And yet... As I look around the Garden, I see something that breaks my heart.
The gardeners don't see the gardens beauty like I do.
They look at flowers that- In their eyes, aren't as beautiful as the others.
They look at the Roses and say, "these ones aren't as beautiful as the others.
So they TRAMPLE.
Or they look at the Daisies and say, "I don't like how these ones are mixed in with the others"
So they SEPERATE.
And for what? their age? their belief? What?
Let me remind you that these aren't flowers that I'm talking about.
All throughout history, we see the exact same problem happening every time.
Wether it be the Jews, The Blacks, The LGBTQ, or Whoever,
Someone is always persecuted and oppressed!
I'm SICK of it! I'm sure you are too!
I know we can be better than this! So let us be better!
Let us not be fools!
